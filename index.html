Introduction

The purpose of this application is to provide a platform where a user can practice conjugating verbs and translating words as fast as possible. The application delivers the content in order of frequency of use and ease of learning. Each sebsequent level in a game presents content that is more difficult or less common. By progressing through the levels, the intention is that a user will gradually deepen their knowledge of vocabulary and grammar. The app is functional and dificult and is designed for serious language learners who are trying to learn the content as fast as possible. The app is currently in construction and only supports Portuguese.
